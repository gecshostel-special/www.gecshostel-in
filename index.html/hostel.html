Hostel Overview

Our hostel provides safe, secure and affordable accommodation for students of Government Engineering College. Booking can be initiated from the homepage under Room Availability & Booking.

Facilities

  • 24x7 Security and CCTV surveillance
  • Wi‑Fi enabled study rooms
  • RO drinking water and hot water access
  • Shared laundry area
  • Mess with vegetarian meals (see Fees for charges)

Floors & Room Types

Floors

  • Floor 4: Single & Double rooms
  • Floor 3: Double rooms
  • Floor 2: Double & Triple rooms
  • Floor 1: Triple rooms

How to Book

  1. Go to Home → Room Availability & Booking
  2. Select floor and room type, then choose a room
  3. Enter student details and confirm

Rules

About the Hostel

Comfortable accommodation with secure premises, 24x7 electricity, purified water, Wi‑Fi, and study-friendly environment.

Facilities

  • Wi‑Fi and study rooms
  • RO drinking water and hot water
  • Indoor and outdoor recreation
  • 24x7 security and CCTV
  • Medical assistance and warden on duty

Floors & Rooms

Floors 1–4 with single, double and triple sharing rooms. Check availability and book from the Home page.

Mess

Nutritious vegetarian meals served three times a day. Mess committee includes student representatives.

Rules

  • Maintain discipline and cleanliness
